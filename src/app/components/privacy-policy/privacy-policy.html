LLC Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

OON SOFT LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide: Name, email address, billing details, communications, and any other information you voluntarily submit.
  • Information collected automatically:IP address, browser type, device details, log data, cookies, and tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver and improve our services and digital product licenses.
  • Process payments securely through trusted third-party payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, 2Checkout, Payoneer, or others).
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Conduct analytics to improve our website and services.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security.
  • Send marketing communications where permitted by law (you may opt out at any time).

You may opt out of receiving marketing emails at any time by clicking the 'unsubscribe' link in our emails or by contacting support@oonsoft.llc.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent (for marketing and non-essential cookies).
  • The necessity to perform a contract (to deliver products/services you purchase).
  • Our legitimate interests (to improve services, prevent fraud, and ensure security).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account remains active
  • Billing and transaction data is retained for at least seven years to comply with legal requirements.
  • Log data is retained for shorter periods unless needed for security or legal purposes.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics services, advertising networks, and legal authorities if required.
  • Trusted third-party providers (such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or others) for analytics and marketing. These third parties may set cookies or track browsing behavior; see our Cookie Policy for details.

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (for website analytics) – https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Facebook Pixel (for advertising and remarketing) – https://www.facebook.com/policy.php
  • Stripe (for payment processing) – https://stripe.com/privacy
  • PayPal (for payment processing) – https://www.paypal.com/privacy
  • 2Checkout (for payment processing) – https://www.2checkout.com/legal/privacy/
  • Payoneer (for payment processing) – https://www.payoneer.com/legal/privacy-policy/

International Data Transfers

If you access our services outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. We use safeguards such as GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses for such transfers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain processing or marketing communications.
  • Exercise rights under GDPR (EU/EEA users) or CCPA (California users), including the right to know, delete, or opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@oonsoft.llc with the subject line “Privacy Request.” We may verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

8. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their information. If you believe a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable laws, typically within 72 hours, and provide information on any recommended protective measures.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.
