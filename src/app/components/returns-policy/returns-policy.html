Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

At OON SOFT LLC, we are committed to providing quality services and products. Digital services such as subscriptions and social media packages are non-refundable once delivered or once a renewal has begun. Customers may cancel subscriptions prior to renewal to avoid future charges.

Refunds for POS software subscriptions are available only if requested within seven days of the initial purchase.

For IT products, returns are accepted within fourteen days of delivery provided the items are unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product is defective, in which case we will cover the return.
