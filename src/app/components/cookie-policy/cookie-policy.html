Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how OON SOFT LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It is part of our Privacy Policy and applies to all visitors of our site.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device, remember your preferences, and enhance your browsing experience. Cookies can be “first-party” (set by us) or “third-party” (set by external services we use).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies:Necessary for the website to function (e.g., login, security, shopping cart).
  • Functional Cookies:Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies:Help us understand how visitors use our website.
  • Marketing Cookies:Used to deliver relevant advertising and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers, payment processors, or advertising networks) that also set cookies on your device when you use our website. These third-party cookies are managed by their respective providers, and we do not control their use. Please refer to the privacy and cookie policies of these third-party services for more information.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through:

  • Our Cookie Consent Manager (if available on our website).
  • Your browser settings (instructions are typically found in your browser’s “Help” section).

Please note that essential cookies cannot be disabled, as they are necessary for the website to operate. Disabling other cookies may affect your experience and limit certain features.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The effective date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated. Please review this policy regularly.
